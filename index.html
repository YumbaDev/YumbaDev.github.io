About Me

Hey I'm Hector and I'm a sophomore this fall semester at the University of Arkansas with plans of graduating with a Computer Science degree. Ever since I can remember, video games played a big role in my life; always fascinated with the prospect of simply popping a cartridge into a console to then entering countless unique worlds and levels, it seemed like magic to me. No matter how many times I rescued Dream Land from King Dedede, beaten the Elite Four to become Pokemon Champion, saved the Solana Galaxy from Dr. Nefarious, I was always compelled by not only the story of each respective game, but moreso how the games themselves were made. My interest in the creation of video games peaked my interest in high school as it drove me to take my first programming class to finally understand. This experience introduced me to the computer science as it showed me the countless oppurtinities I could have when it came to creating something, not only video games.
